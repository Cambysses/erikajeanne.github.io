Hi I'm Erika!

An illustrator and maker based right here in the maritimes: Halifax, Nova Scotia. My work primarily focuses on comfy pop culture, video games, classic animated movies and originals that feel like a warm hug or long walk in the woods!

I've been vending my art at events since 2018; always growing and bringing fresh new merch to each show I have the pleasure of attending. This year my focus is to go back to my artist roots with traditional media: watercolour, gouache, mix media collage and physical one-of-a-kind fibre pieces.

Below is a catalog of my current available products and a sneak peak of WIP for the future.

Thank you for your consideration and time! y'all are da best!

Stickers! 💖

All my stickers are made in-house (my house aha!). They are all dishwasher and water bottle safe. Below is a mix of pop culture and original designs.

Sticker Packs & Sheets! 💖

Because sometimes one isn't enough; these are a collection of the same in-house stickers as above, usually in smaller sizes - easy to share!

Prints! 💖

All my prints come in either 8x10 or 5x7 - both photos sizes for easy framing. I have two options: archival paper, or cardstock for a more accessible price option. These are printed in-house as well.

Everything else! 💖

Last year I had the opportunity to order merchandise via a group order with other local artists; these large keychains, or bag charms, are the result.

I also have a select amount of designs on screens to be printed on tote bags and clothings.
