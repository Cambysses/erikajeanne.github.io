Hi I'm Erika!

A kitsch focused artist and illustrator based out of Halifax Nova Scotia.

I love getting to interact with patrons at fan run events to connect over our common interests; I've had a wonderful time tabling at events the for the past 5-6 years!

My work focuses on family friendly shows, fandoms & original art that feels like a warm hug.

Thank you for your consideration and for hosting such a great event here in the maritimes!
